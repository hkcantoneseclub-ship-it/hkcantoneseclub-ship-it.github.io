隱私政策

最後更新日期：2026年7月8日

感謝您使用「Personalities Test: 16型人格測試」（以下簡稱「本應用程式」）。我們非常重視您的隱私權，本隱私政策說明我們如何收集、使用及保護您的資訊。

1. 我們收集的資訊

1.1 本機儲存資訊（不會離開您的裝置）

本應用程式使用裝置的本機儲存空間（SharedPreferences）保存以下資訊，這些資訊僅儲存在您的裝置上，不會被上傳至任何伺服器：

  • 語言偏好設定
  • 測驗進度與答題紀錄
  • 歷史測驗結果

1.2 廣告相關資訊（由第三方收集）

本應用程式使用 Google AdMob 提供廣告服務。AdMob 可能會收集以下資訊以提供個人化或非個人化廣告：

資訊類型用途
廣告識別碼（Advertising ID）用於廣告投放與成效分析
裝置資訊（型號、作業系統版本）確保廣告正常顯示
概略位置資訊（如國家/地區）提供符合地區的廣告內容
應用程式使用資料廣告投放優化與防止詐欺

詳情請參閱 Google 廣告政策 及 Google 隱私權政策。

2. 我們如何使用資訊

  • 提供及改善人格測驗功能
  • 儲存您的測驗歷史紀錄以供日後查看
  • 透過 AdMob 顯示廣告以維持本應用程式的免費營運

3. 資訊分享

我們不會出售、交易或轉讓您的個人資訊給第三方，惟以下情況除外：

  • Google AdMob 廣告服務所需的資訊收集（依其自身隱私政策運作）
  • 法律要求或保護我們權利所必需的情況

4. 兒童隱私

本應用程式不會刻意向 13 歲以下兒童收集個人資訊。若您發現您的孩子向我們提供了個人資訊，請透過下方聯絡方式與我們聯繫，我們將盡快刪除相關資料。

5. 資料安全

本機儲存的資料受您裝置本身的安全機制保護。我們不會將您的測驗答案或結果傳送至任何外部伺服器。

6. 您的權利與選擇

  • 您可以在裝置設定中隨時重設廣告識別碼或選擇退出個人化廣告
  • 您可以透過解除安裝本應用程式來刪除所有本機儲存的測驗資料

7. 政策變更

我們可能不時更新本隱私政策，任何變更將反映在此頁面上，並更新「最後更新日期」。建議您定期查閱本頁面以了解最新資訊。

Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 8, 2026

Thank you for using "Personalities Test: 16型人格測試" (the "App"). We take your privacy seriously. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Locally Stored Information (never leaves your device)

The App uses your device's local storage (SharedPreferences) to save the following information, which is stored only on your device and never uploaded to any server:

  • Language preference
  • Quiz progress and answers
  • Historical test results

1.2 Advertising-Related Information (collected by third parties)

The App uses Google AdMob to serve advertisements. AdMob may collect the following information to provide personalized or non-personalized ads:

Data TypePurpose
Advertising IDAd delivery and performance measurement
Device information (model, OS version)Ensuring proper ad rendering
Approximate location (e.g., country/region)Providing region-appropriate ad content
App usage dataAd optimization and fraud prevention

For more details, please refer to Google's Advertising Policies and Google's Privacy Policy.

2. How We Use Information

  • To provide and improve the personality quiz functionality
  • To store your quiz history for later viewing
  • To display ads via AdMob to support the App's free operation

3. Information Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or transfer your personal information to third parties, except:

  • Information collection required by Google AdMob's advertising services (governed by its own privacy policy)
  • Where required by law or to protect our rights

4. Children's Privacy

The App does not knowingly collect personal information from children under the age of 13. If you believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us using the information below, and we will promptly delete such data.

5. Data Security

Locally stored data is protected by your device's own security mechanisms. We do not transmit your quiz answers or results to any external server.

6. Your Rights and Choices

  • You may reset your Advertising ID or opt out of personalized ads at any time in your device settings
  • You may delete all locally stored quiz data by uninstalling the App

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page along with an updated "Last updated" date. We encourage you to review this page periodically.
